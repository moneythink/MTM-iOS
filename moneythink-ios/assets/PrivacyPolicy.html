If you have any questions or comments about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at hello@moneythink.org.

Children under the Age of 13

Our Website and App are not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. No one under age 13 may provide any PII (i.e., any information that may be used to specifically identify or contact you, such as your name, address, e-mail address, phone number, etc.) or other information on the Website or App. We do not knowingly collect PII or other information from children under 13. If you are under 13, do not use or provide any PII or other information on the Website or App. If we learn we have collected or received PII or other information from a child under 13, we will delete that PII or other information. If you believe we might have any PII or other information from or about a child under 13, please contact us using the details set out above.

The Website and App typically collect two kinds of information about you: (a) PII; and (b) non-personal information (i.e., information that does not personally identify you which we automatically collect when you visit the Website or App or that you provide to us, including certain PII that has been rendered anonymous) (“Non-PII”). Other Uses & Information

IP Addresses: An IP address is a number that is automatically assigned to your computer whenever you are surfing the Internet. Web servers (computers that "serve up" web pages) automatically identify your computer by its IP address. When visitors request pages from our Website or App, our servers typically log their IP addresses. We collect IP addresses for purposes of system administration, to report non-personal aggregate information to others, and to track the use of our Website or App. IP addresses are considered non-personal information and may also be shared as provided above.

Cookies: "Cookies" are small text files from a website that are stored on your hard drive. These text files make using our Website and App more convenient by, among other things, saving your passwords and preferences for you. You may refuse to accept browser cookies by activating the appropriate setting on your browser. You may also delete a cookie manually from your hard drive through your internet browser or other programs. Please note, however, that some parts of our Website and App may not function properly or be available to you if you refuse to accept a cookie or choose to disable the acceptance of cookies.
